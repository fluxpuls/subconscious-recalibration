# Subconscious Recalibration

## Overview

This repository focuses on non-discursive protocol positive visualization approaches designed to help individuals optimize their mental models. The method leverages the subconscious mind's natural ability to self-correct when negative patterns are isolated and visualized without analysis or discussion of underlying causes.

## How It Works

Unlike traditional talk therapy, this approach avoids discussing past events or analyzing the story behind blocks. Instead, it uses a guided relaxation state combined with specific energy visualization to reset the subconscious system to its optimal baseline.

## The Philosophy

Based on scientific research, the human mind operates on relative, not absolute, scales. Negative experiences act as static that skew this scale. By entering a relaxed state and visualizing energy flow, we can decouple these patterns from the mental model, allowing the system to self-correct to its natural equilibrium.
